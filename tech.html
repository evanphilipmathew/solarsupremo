Technical Specifications of the Solar Supremo Drone

Working Principle

Designing a solar-powered drone requires a careful balance of power and weight, utilizing efficient materials and configurations.

Power System

Component Details
Solar Cells Voltage: 4V per cell, Number: 7 (28V in series)
Battery Type: LiPo, Voltage: 12V, Capacity: 1000mAh

Motors and Propulsion

Component Details
Motors Type: 4 BLDC motors, 12V, 0.5A each
Propellers Material: Carbon fiber, Reusable

Control System

Includes a flight controller compatible with the drone’s configuration, a power distribution module to distribute power, and a radio control system (transmitter and receiver).

Camera and FPV

FPV Camera: Lightweight, low power, standard FPV frequencies for video transmission.

Frame

Material: Hard cardboard and popsicle sticks, reinforced for stability. Weight: 1.3 kg.

Approximate Cost

4000 INR (~198 AED, ~$54 USD)

Flight Time and Payload Capacity

Flight Time: 10-15 minutes. Payload Capacity: 100-200g.

Drone Parts and Functions

Solar Cells: Mounted on the drone to harness solar energy for direct charging or battery storage.

BLDC Motors: Four 12V motors, each rated at 0.5A.

Flight Controller: Manages flight stability and control.

Power Distribution Module: Distributes power to the motors and other components.

Transmitter and Receiver: Radio control system for operating the drone.

FPV Camera: Lightweight camera with video transmission capabilities.

Future Considerations

Safety

  • Fail-Safe Measures: Automatic landing when battery is low, and return-to-home functionality for loss of connection or low power.
  • Obstacle Avoidance: Integration of ultrasonic or lidar sensors to detect and avoid obstacles.

GPS Connection

  • GPS Module: For precise flight control and position tracking, enabling waypoint navigation and geofencing.

Data Logging

  • Telemetry: Track and record flight data such as GPS coordinates, altitude, speed, and battery level.

Improved Flight Efficiency

  • Solar Cell Optimization: Use of high-efficiency solar cells to extend flight time.
  • Weight Management: Use lighter but durable materials for the frame.

Enhanced Control and Communication

  • Long-Range Communication: Upgrades to transmitter and receiver for extended range.
  • Live Video Streaming: Improved FPV camera for clearer video streaming.

Advanced Power Management
  • Battery Management System: Features to protect and monitor the battery’s health and performance.

Regulations and Compliance

  • Adherence to Regulations: Compliance with local and international laws, ensuring safe operation.
